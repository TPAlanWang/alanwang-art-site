關於/ About

往返於台北與台中的爵士小號演奏者。
深受Clifford Brown, Fabrizio Bosso影響，演奏風格融合了傳統 Hard Bop 的律動與現代即興的色彩。
在舞台上湧動奔流的旋律，與隨煙霧飄揚的茶香之間，尋找流動的平靜；
在標準曲的曲式與嚴謹的服裝版形結構中，探索自由穿梭的路徑。

A jazz trumpeter moving between Taipei and Taichung.
Deeply influenced by Clifford Brown and Fabrizio Bosso,
his playing blends the rhythmic drive of traditional hard bop with the colors of modern improvisation.
Between the surging flow of melody on stage and the drifting aroma of tea carried in the air, he seeks a calm in motion—
and within the forms of jazz standards and the rigor of tailored garment structures, he explores paths through which freedom can quietly pass.
